Visualization Options

Six approaches for the brain activity panel. Sample: orange (idx 124).

A: Animated Waveforms

The original style. 8 visual cortex channels on a dark background, scrolling in real time. Clean and techy.

Brain Activity

B: Waveforms with Glowing Peaks

Same waveform style, but the P100 and N170 peaks pulse with light when the brain "recognizes" the object. Like a heartbeat monitor moment.

Brain Activity — peaks highlighted

C: Interactive Head Map

Click a brain region to see its waveforms. Electrode dots pulse with signal strength. Dark waveform panel on the right.

Visual Cortex

D: Warm-Palette Explorer

Same interactive head map concept, but the head uses your site's warm cream/amber palette instead of clinical colors. Click a region to explore.

Visual Cortex

E: Cinematic Sequence

"Watch a brain see." The image flashes, waveforms ripple with glowing peaks, the reconstruction emerges from blur, and the score counts up. Loops every 4 seconds.

0 ms

F: 3D Rotating Brain

A slowly rotating 3D brain with electrode positions glowing based on signal amplitude. The visual cortex lights up as data plays. Drag to rotate.
